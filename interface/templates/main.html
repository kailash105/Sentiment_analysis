Sentimental Analysis

Sentiment analysis utilizes natural language processing to discern emotions conveyed in text, classifying them as positive, negative, or neutral. It aids in understanding public opinion, guiding decision-making in areas like social media monitoring, market research, and customer feedback analysis. By providing insights into user sentiment, sentiment analysis enables businesses to refine products/services, manage brand reputation, and identify emerging trends. Its application extends across diverse fields, facilitating data-driven strategies and enhancing customer engagement.
